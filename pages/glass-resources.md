Glass Resources H1

Most glass collectors enjoy knowing which glass producer made their piece, but with antique glass this can be tricky as few catalogues survive. However, enthusiasts in the community have made it their life’s work to track these catalogues down and to piece together the history of their glass.

Here are some links to the largest and most comprehensive sites, but this is definitely not an exhaustive list!
