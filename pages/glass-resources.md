Glass Resources H1

Most glass collectors enjoy knowing as much information as possible about their piece, and part of this quest involves finding out who made it. With antique glass, this can be tricky as few catalogues survive, and not all are readily available. However, enthusiasts in the community have made it their life’s work to track these catalogues down and to piece together the history of their glass.

Here are some links to the largest and most comprehensive sites to help on your search for glass identification, but this is definitely not an exhaustive list! do let me know if there's a site or resource you think should be added here.
