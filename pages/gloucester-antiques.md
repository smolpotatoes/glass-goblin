I am proud to have a cabinet in Gloucester Antiques Centre, an amazing historic building situated right in the middle of the city and just a stone’s throw from the cathedral. The Antiques Centre spans three floors and is full to the brim of treasure.

We even have a resident cat, Thumbs. My cabinet is located on the ground floor at the rear of the building and has a large quantity of uranium glass, as well as plenty of other beautiful pieces from around the world.

Buying from the cabinet
The Antiques Centre doesn’t have an online shop, so to buy from the cabinet you either need to visit in person or [drop me a message] to see what’s currently up for grabs.
