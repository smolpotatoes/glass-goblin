Payment plan terms and conditions

Glass Goblin is pleased to offer payment plans to any Buyer who wishes to use them under the following terms and conditions:

Initial deposit
The initial deposit must be at least 10% of the item price or £10, whichever is the greater. This portion of the deposit is non-refundable, but any sum paid as a deposit over the minimum requirements can be returned to the buyer upon cancellation of the sale. Payment of the initial deposit removes the item from general sale and ensures it will not be sold elsewhere.

Payment schedules
The payment schedule is mutually agreed by Glass Goblin and the Buyer, at a rate that is affordable and convenient for both parties. Payment schedules can include weekly or monthly payments, or something else to suit us both. Payment sums can vary providing they are agreed in advance.

Example 1
There is a piece of French opaline uranium for sale at £250 included P&P. The minimum deposit amount would be £25 (although any deposit sum can be paid). Payment terms are agreed as follows:
Initial deposit - £25 plus an extra £25
Four further payments of £50 each
Total payable - £250

Cancellation by Glass Goblin
Glass Goblin endeavours never to cancel a sale. However, glass is known to break from time to time, and in the event that Glass Goblin cannot source a replacement or suitable alternative, a refund will be offered to the Buyer. In the event that the sale is cancelled by Glass Goblin through no fault of the Buyer, 100% of any monies paid to Glass Goblin will be eligible to be refunded. Refunds will be processed within 7 working days.

Missed / defaulted payments
Glass Goblin considers a payment missed if the monies due on an agreed date are not received by 23:59 of the calendar date in the time zone in which the Buyer resides. As part of its commitment to lowering anxiety around household money issues and focusing on joy, a missed payment does not immediately violate the terms of a payment plan and result in cancellation of the purchase, providing either:
The Buyer notified Glass Goblin in advance of the payment due date and arranged an alteration to the schedule
The missed payment is made within 48 hours of 23:59 of the calendar date in the time zone in which the Buyer resides
If neither of these provisions are met, Glass Goblin will cancel the sale.

Glass Goblin hopes never to need to cancel a sale as a result of defaulted or missed payments by the Buyer, but in the event that this becomes necessary, the £10 / 10% deposit will not be refundable and will instead be used to cover administrative costs. Refunds due under £100 will be processed within 7 working days, and refunds due over £100 will be processed within 14 working days. Glass Goblin reserves the right to refuse future payment plans to any Buyer who has previously defaulted on or cancelled a payment plan.

Example 2
There is a vase for sale for £60. The initial deposit would be £10 (as 10% is only £6). If the deposit paid was £30 instead of £10, £20 would be refundable to the Buyer in the event that the Buyer cancelled the sale. If Glass Goblin were to cancel the sale, £30 would be refundable to the Buyer.

General information
A payment plan is not an extension of credit, and Glass Goblin is not a credit broker. Glass Goblin offers payment plans at its sole discretion and reserves the right to refuse any plan or any Buyer. A payment plan is mutually decided and accepted by both the Buyer and Glass Goblin, including the initial deposit amount and the subsequent payment schedule.
