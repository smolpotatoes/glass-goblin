Want to stay in touch?
I’d love that! There are lots of ways to stay in the loop…

Newsletters
When I’m organised enough I’ll send the occasional email newsletter. Leave your email address below and I’ll put you on the list. You can unsubscribe at any time.

WhatsApp
I’m planning to use WhatsApp to notify those who want to know when a special piece is coming up for sale, and perhaps a ‘weekly menu’ of what’s coming up. This hasn’t started yet, but you can leave your number below to be added to my channel. You can take yourself off at any time. https://whatsapp.com/channel/0029VbAnmlqId7nTDqcPfw2K QR code

Facebook / Instagram
Facebook and Insta are great places to see what I’m up to, not least because pieces are released for sale there first, before any other platform! Give me a follow to make sure you’re among the first to know when a new piece is up for grabs.

Etsy / Vinted
For now, Etsy and Vinted are my storefronts, at least until I get organised enough to list everything here. You can follow/favourite my shop so that you’re notified when something new is listed. Anything that isn’t immediately snapped up on my social media goes onto Vinted and Etsy. High value items and handmade jewellery are more likely to be listed on Etsy, bargain buys and sale pieces usually go on Vinted.
