My journey into glass
I first started collecting glass a few years ago when I found out about uranium glass - I was instantly hooked. However, I've loved antiques my whole life and have always shopped in charity shops and auction houses, ever since I had pocket money to spend.

Eventually, my own glass collection got a bit out of hand and I decided to find some of it a new home. At the same time, I was desperately unhappy in my day job, and after a lot of soul searching, decided to take a big old risk and sell antique glass full time. I haven't looked back!
